## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: "El Equipo Insatisfecho"
**Who:** Small to mid-size agency (3–15 agents), based in CABA or GBA, currently on Tokko Broker for 12+ months.

**Firmographics:**
- 3–15 licensed agents (corredores)
- CABA, GBA, or major provincial capital (Rosario, Córdoba, Mendoza)
- Active on 2+ portals (Zonaprop + at least one other)
- 50–500 active listings
- Revenue: agency closing 5–30 operations/month

**Technographics:**
- Uses Tokko as primary CRM
- Uses WhatsApp Business (separate from Tokko) for client comms
- Has at least 1 agent complaining about Tokko's mobile experience

**Psychographics:**
- Agency owner is 30–50 years old, growth-minded
- Has tried pushing agents to use Tokko more systematically, with partial success
- Frustrated by "leads arriving late" or "agents not updating the CRM"
- Not primarily price-sensitive — willing to pay for something that actually works

**Why they convert fastest:**
- Clear pain (WhatsApp gap, mobile gap, stale leads)
- Decision maker is reachable and has budget authority
- Team size is small enough that onboarding is fast
- Annual contract value justifies sales effort

### Secondary ICP: "El Broker Independiente"
**Who:** Solo broker or duo, not using any CRM, managing everything in WhatsApp + Google Sheets.

**Why:** Greenfield acquisition, no switching cost. Land cheap (free or low plan), expand as they grow. High volume, low ACV per deal — needs self-serve or low-touch sales.

### Do Not Target (for now)
- Agencies using HubSpot or Salesforce (too much customization, too long a sales cycle)
- Franchisees (RE/MAX, Century 21) — franchise contracts often mandate specific software
- Agencies with fewer than 5 transactions/month — too low LTV to justify CAC

## 2. Discovery Questions

Use these to qualify a prospect and understand their pain before pitching.

### Tier 1 — Qualify fast (first 5 minutes)
1. "¿Cuántos agentes tienen actualmente en la inmobiliaria?"
2. "¿Qué software o CRM usan hoy para gestionar propiedades y contactos?"
3. "¿Publican en portales como Zonaprop o MercadoLibre? ¿Cuántos portales activos tienen?"

*If they have <3 agents and no software → route to self-serve. If they use Tokko → primary ICP confirmed. If they use something else → note and continue.*

### Tier 2 — Diagnose pain (minutes 5–15)
4. "¿Cómo reciben las consultas de los portales? ¿Las reciben directo en Tokko o también por WhatsApp?"
5. "¿Hay un proceso para que los agentes registren los seguimientos en el CRM, o cada uno lo hace a su manera?"
6. "¿Usan WhatsApp para hablar con clientes? ¿Cómo lo manejan con el equipo?"
7. "¿Qué tanto usan el celular para trabajar — propiedades, clientes, oportunidades?"
8. "¿Alguna vez perdieron un cliente porque respondieron tarde o se cayó la información en algún lugar?"

*Red flags (= strong buy signal): leads by WhatsApp separate from CRM, agents not updating records, leads arriving with delay, no mobile access.*

### Tier 3 — Budget & decision (minutes 15–20, for qualified prospects)
9. "¿Quién en la inmobiliaria toma la decisión de adoptar una herramienta nueva?"
10. "¿Tienen presupuesto para software de gestión? ¿Saben cuánto están pagando por Tokko ahora?"
11. "Si encontraran algo que resuelve [X pain], ¿cuánto tiempo tardarían en tomar una decisión?"

## 3. Demo Script — 15-Minute Flow

**Goal:** Show the moment-of-truth that makes them say "this is what I've been missing."
**Lead with the WhatsApp/inbox problem** — that's the #1 pain point. Everything else supports.

### Pre-demo setup
- Have a fresh agency demo environment loaded
- Have 2–3 sample properties in the inventory (use a Buenos Aires neighborhood they know)
- Have a fake "lead" ready to fire during the demo
- Know which portals they use before the demo starts

---

### Flow

**[0:00–1:00] Hook — their biggest pain**
> "Antes de empezar, quiero mostrarte lo que más le molesta a las inmobiliarias que nos contactan. ¿Me dijiste que usan WhatsApp para hablar con clientes, verdad?"

Confirm yes → "Perfecto. Arrancamos por ahí."

---

**[1:00–3:00] Unified Inbox — WhatsApp + portal consults**
- Open the Inbox view
- Show a WhatsApp message from a lead, a Zonaprop consult, and an email — all in one timeline
- "Acá ves todas las consultas juntas — WhatsApp, Zonaprop, correo. Un solo lugar. Sin copiar y pegar."
- Trigger a live demo lead to arrive (pre-scheduled)
- Show the notification arriving in real time: "¿Ves? Llegó al instante. Sin los 2 minutos de espera de Tokko."

**Pause and ask:** "¿Cuántas consultas les entran por día entre todos los portales más WhatsApp?"

---

**[3:00–5:00] Properties — the import and the listing**
- Open Properties list
- Show a property card — stable code that never changes, clean UI
- "Si en Tokko el código cambia cuando la propiedad pasa de estado, con nosotros es siempre el mismo. El cliente siempre puede encontrarla."
- Show the AI description generator: upload 2 photos → watch the description auto-generate in Spanish
- "30 minutos de trabajo de redacción, resuelto en 15 segundos."

---

**[5:00–7:00] Portal publish**
- Show the portal publishing screen
- "Zonaprop, Argenprop, MercadoLibre — con un click, todos actualizados."
- Show the publication status per portal with live sync indicator
- "Y si algo falla en un portal, lo ves acá — no te enterás por el cliente."

---

**[7:00–9:00] Lead arrives → Kanban pipeline**
- The demo lead we triggered earlier is now in the pipeline
- Show the Kanban view — stages mirror the familiar 8-stage Tokko pipeline
- Assign to an agent, add a note, log a follow-up
- "El agente lo ve en su celular al instante." → switch to mobile view
- Show the PWA mobile: same pipeline, same properties, offline-ready
- "Desde el celular, igual que en la pantalla grande."

---

**[9:00–11:00] AI-suggested reply**
- Open the lead's inbox thread
- Show the AI draft reply button: "Escribir respuesta con IA"
- Generate a draft based on the property the lead asked about
- "El agente revisa, edita si quiere, y lo manda. 30 segundos en lugar de 5 minutos."

---

**[11:00–13:00] Commission split (if applicable)**
- Show a deal closing — reservation → commission split between agents
- "Cuando se cierra la operación, la comisión se calcula y se distribuye automáticamente."
- Show the escritura/boleto document workflow
- "El flujo de documentos completo — reserva, boleto, escritura — todo en el sistema."

---

**[13:00–15:00] Close — their team on it**
- "¿Cuántos agentes tienen que manejar esto?" → frame the team size
- "Les podemos tener el equipo entero funcionando en [1–2 días]. La migración desde Tokko la hacemos nosotros."
- "¿Qué preguntas tienen?"

**If they're excited:** Schedule a trial access / onboarding call before leaving the conversation.

## 5. Competitive Battlecard — Corredor vs Tokko

| Dimension | Tokko Broker | Corredor |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **WhatsApp integration** | ❌ None | ✅ Unified inbox — WhatsApp + portals + email |
| **Mobile** | ❌ No app, poor mobile web | ✅ PWA — full functionality on any device |
| **Lead notification speed** | ⚠️ 120-second polling delay | ✅ Instant (WebSockets/SSE) |
| **AI features** | ❌ None | ✅ Description autofill, email drafts, lead triage |
| **Property codes** | ⚠️ Mutate on status change | ✅ Stable UUIDs, never change |
| **Security** | ⚠️ JWTs in URLs, eval() on responses | ✅ HttpOnly cookies, no JWTs in URLs, strict CSP |
| **Analytics** | ⚠️ Table-style reports only | ✅ Cohort, funnel, commission forecast |
| **Portals supported** | Zonaprop, Argenprop, MercadoLibre, Proppit + others | Same + expanding |
| **Multi-branch** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| **Document workflow** | ✅ Reserva/Boleto/Escritura | ✅ Same + e-sign |
| **Pricing transparency** | ❌ No public pricing, negotiation required | ✅ Public tiers, self-serve |
| **Free trial** | ❌ Demo only, sales-gated | ✅ Self-serve trial |
| **Data export** | ⚠️ CSV only, partial | ✅ Full export any time |
| **Tech stack age** | 18 years, Django + jQuery | ✅ Modern (2026), single SPA |
| **Market tenure** | 18 years — known, trusted | 🆕 New — requires trust building |

**Our strengths:** WhatsApp, mobile, AI, speed, security, pricing transparency.
**Their strengths:** Market familiarity, 18 years of customer relationships, chamber endorsements.

## 7. Sales Process & Stage Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Key action |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Prospecting** | Identified agency, not yet contacted | Research decision maker, personalize first touch |
| **Contacted** | First outreach sent or made | Follow up within 48h |
| **Discovery** | Had qualifying conversation | Log pain points, send discovery recap email |
| **Demo** | Demo scheduled or completed | Send recap + next steps within 24h |
| **Trial** | Access granted, team onboarding | Check in at day 3 and day 7 |
| **Proposal** | Pricing sent | Follow up in 48h; surface blockers |
| **Negotiation** | Active back-and-forth | Involve CMO if pricing flexibility needed; CEO if >$50k ACV |
| **Closed Won** | Contract signed | Hand off to CS for onboarding |
| **Closed Lost** | Not moving forward | Log reason; set 6-month check-in |

### Escalation rules
- ACV > USD 50k/year → Board escalation before proposal sign-off (per agent instructions)
- Any deal requiring product commitments → escalate to CEO before promising
- Any security audit requests → escalate to CTO

## 8. First 30 Days — Outbound Launch Plan

### Week 1: Targeting list build
- Pull CUCICBA registered agency list (public via CUCICBA.com.ar or scrape)
- Enrich with LinkedIn for agency owner/decision maker
- Filter: agencies with Zonaprop/Argenprop presence (= they have listings volume and portal budget)
- Target: 200 accounts in CABA and GBA Norte (Zona Norte has high-value agencies)
- Tool: Apollo.io or manual LinkedIn + Zonaprop cross-reference

### Week 2: First outreach wave
- 200 cold emails/LinkedIn DMs — personalized by neighborhood + portal presence
- Subject line test A: "La consulta de Zonaprop que llegó tarde" vs B: "WhatsApp + Zonaprop en un solo lugar"
- Follow-up sequence: day 1 → day 4 → day 8 → day 14

### Week 3: Demo calls
- Target: 10–15 demos from 200 outreach (5–7% connect rate)
- Prioritize agencies that opened email 2+ times or visited the site
- Book demos via Calendly; send prep email 24h before

### Week 4: Referral activation
- For any agency that demoed (even those who didn't buy): ask for 2 referrals
- "¿Conoces otras inmobiliarias que estén con los mismos problemas con Tokko?"
- Offer referral credit (1 month free) for any referred agency that signs
